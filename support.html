FAQ

Q: Why do I need to grant Screen Time permission?
blankr uses Apple's Screen Time APIs to manage app access during your scheduled focus periods. This permission is required for the app to function.
Q: Can I use blankr on the Simulator?
No. Screen Time features require a physical iOS device.
Q: My schedule didn't activate. What should I do?
Make sure Screen Time authorization is enabled in Settings > Screen Time. Then reopen blankr and check that your schedule is saved and toggled on.
Q: How do I cancel a timer early?
Open blankr and tap the active timer on the Home screen. Depending on your resistance level, you may need to complete a waiting period before ending early.
Q: Does blankr work when the app is closed?
Yes. Schedules are enforced by a system extension that runs independently of the main app.
Q: How do breaks work?
During an active schedule, you can request a break to temporarily lift app restrictions. When the break expires, restrictions are automatically re-applied.
