# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 25, 2026

blankr ("the App") is developed by StillLab. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.** The App operates entirely on your device.

### On-Device Data

The App stores the following data locally on your device to function:

- **Schedules and settings** — your blocking schedules, break configurations, and preferences are stored in on-device shared storage (UserDefaults via App Group) so the app and its extensions can coordinate.
- **Override key count** — stored in the device Keychain and iCloud Key-Value Storage so your purchased keys persist across reinstalls and devices signed into the same Apple ID.
- **Streak and usage data** — session completion dates are stored locally to calculate your streak. This data never leaves your device.

### Apple Screen Time (FamilyControls)

The App uses Apple's FamilyControls, DeviceActivity, and ManagedSettings frameworks to block selected apps during scheduled times. **The App never sees or accesses the names, bundle identifiers, or usage data of your apps.** Apple's Screen Time APIs use opaque tokens — the App can only pass these tokens back to the system to apply or remove restrictions.

### In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. The App does not receive or store any payment information, billing details, or Apple ID information.

### Analytics and Tracking

The App does **not** include any analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, advertising frameworks, or tracking of any kind. No data is shared with third parties.

## iCloud

The App uses iCloud Key-Value Storage solely to sync your override key count across your devices. No other data is stored in iCloud.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. All data remains on-device and is managed through Apple's parental control frameworks.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date.
